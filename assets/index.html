Cry Baby EP

This album tells the story of Cry Baby, a fantasy version of Melanie Martinez when she was a child, and a representation of her vulnerable and messed up side. Melanie claims that many of the things that have happened to Cry Baby are similar to the things that have happened in her own life, [2] except where she gets kidnapped and where she kills her kidnapped.

Pity Party EP

The EP was announced on April 27th, 2016 by PopBuzz, but the date was never confirmed by Melanie herself. The EP serves as an international version of the previous Dollhouse EP, as only the US got the Dollhouse EP, therefore Dead to Me and Bittersweet Tragedy were previously only available in the United States.
